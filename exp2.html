The Toyota Supra is a sports car and grand tourer manufactured by the Toyota Motor Corporation beginning in 1978. The name "supra" is derived from the Latin prefix, meaning "above", "to surpass" or "go beyond".

The 2023 Toyota Supra reigns supreme when it comes to a total package sports car. While there are other supple and more spacious options in this segment, the Supra is a renaissance car available at a budget-friendly price point.
